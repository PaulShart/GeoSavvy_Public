Community Guidelines

1. Be Respectful

Treat others with kindness and respect. No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination.

2. Keep Content Appropriate

Quizzes must be suitable for all ages. No offensive, political, or inappropriate content.

3. Create Quality Quizzes

Ensure quizzes are accurate, relevant, and well-structured. Avoid misleading titles or incorrect information.

4. Give Constructive Reviews

Rate quizzes fairly (1–5 stars) based on content and experience. Use reviews to offer helpful feedback

5. No Spam or Plagiarism

Don’t copy content from others without credit. Avoid repetitive or promotional content.

6. Follow the Rules

Repeated violations may result in content removal or account suspension.
